DATA SOURCES & ACCURACY

FAA LAANC AIRSPACE
   Source: FAA UAS Facility Maps (official)
   Accuracy: ±100 feet typical
   Update: Monthly
   License: Public Domain

FAA DIGITAL OBSTACLE FILE
   Source: FAA DOF (official) — obstacles > 200 ft AGL
   Accuracy: ±20–100 feet (FAA-verified)
   Update: Continuous
   License: Public Domain

CELL TOWERS (OpenCelliD)
   Source: https://opencellid.org (crowdsourced)

   Update: Real-time
   License: CC BY 4.0

NO-FLY ZONES (OpenStreetMap)
   Source: Parks, protected areas, water bodies
   Accuracy: Varies (good for parks, ~±100–500m)
   Update: Real-time
   License: ODbL

AIRFIELDS (OpenStreetMap)
   Source: Airports, helipads, airfields
   Accuracy: ±100–500 meters
   Update: Real-time
   License: ODbL

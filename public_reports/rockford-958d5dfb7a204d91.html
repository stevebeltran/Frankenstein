Executive Summary
Deployment recommendation for Nashville, TN
This analysis modeled Drone as First Responder deployment for Nashville, TN using jurisdiction-specific geography and incident demand. The recommended configuration of 14 responder aircraft and 1 guardian aircraft is projected to cover 59.3% of modeled calls, reduce average response time by 1.7 minutes, and improve aerial first-arrival consistency across the jurisdiction.
